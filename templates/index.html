Technology and Inventions

Introduction

Technology has always played a crucial role in shaping human civilization. From the wheel to the internet, each invention has propelled society forward, transforming the way we live, work, and interact.

Historical Milestones

  • The Wheel: Revolutionized transportation and commerce.
  • The Printing Press: Spread knowledge and literacy.
  • The Steam Engine: Fueled the Industrial Revolution.
  • The Telephone: Transformed communication.
  • The Internet: Connected the world.
